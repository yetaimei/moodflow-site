Frequently asked questions

What is MoodFlow?

MoodFlow is a mood tracker and emotion journal for iPhone. It is built for daily mood tracking, helping people log feelings quickly and understand emotional patterns over time.

How is MoodFlow different from a basic mood tracker?

A basic mood tracker often records a simple mood score. MoodFlow adds richer emotion labels, short notes, and a more visual emotion journal experience so people can understand more than a one-word check-in.

Can I track detailed emotions, not just happy or sad?

Yes. MoodFlow is designed for more expressive emotional tags, so you can track detailed emotions like calm, grateful, anxious, relieved, or overwhelmed.

Does MoodFlow show emotional patterns over time?

Yes. MoodFlow helps users see emotional patterns over time with a monthly view, daily logs, and visual summaries that make changes easier to notice.
